What Sli Fox is

At Sli Fox we take forums and our beloved gamers and combine them into a big community where everyone helps each other out in games respectfully. If you're having a problem in a game where you can't pass a certain mission or if you want to obtain the certain achievements then Sli Fox is the place for you. Think of IGN and all of those youtube tutorials combined together to make Sli Fox. You will be able to create your own forums and account in order to contribute to the community of forums.

List of things you can do in the Sli Fox community:

  • Give feedback on completing missions and achievements in games
  • Petition for new games to be released
  • Rate games based on their cover pics (coming soon)
